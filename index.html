Discussion

The vision here is aligned with what I believe is the most interesting research direction in LLMs for “machine learners”: Instead of fixing or improving LLMs, how can we leverage them to make better ML methodology? A few examples:

1) Using LLMs for causal inference:

  • Infer treatment effects from reports in social media (Nikita’s work).
  • Imagine (confounder) covariates for causal effect estimation (predictor is Y(x)Y(x)Y(x)﻿ instead of Y∣X=xY|X=xY∣X=x﻿).

2) LLMs as randomized classifiers: instead of sampling predictions, sample classifiers, e.g. a set of rules. You can then take multiple samples and let them vote. This can significantly (in theory) enhance the accuracy.

3) Using LLMs for performative prediction: Can it simulate feedback loops and estimate the equilibrium of predictions?

4) How can we improve Deep Language Networks?

  • Improve the design of F(⋅,π)\mathtt{F}(\cdot, \pi)F(⋅,π)﻿. We don’t want certain choices to bias the predictions, e.g. position of answers in multiple-choice questions. Can the LLM inspect this? Prediction should be invariant to changes in text that do not change its meaning.
  • Clear that the optimization of π\piπ﻿ can be improved.

Extrapolation, Covariate shift, hard tasks, etc

2 cases:

1) Covariate shift between LLM training and our task.

2) Covariate shift between ICL examples and our task.

3) Both.

Idea:

Task is to predict Y from X. Let’s say Y is discrete and finite.

Define a rv Z s.t. you think Y_x indep of X given Z. AKA Z is a confounder of X and Y.You can think of Z as an environment that X was sampled from.

You are given x and want to predict y^\hat{y}y^​﻿.

a) Ask the LLM to imagine what could be the z of x. (temperature 0.8 or 0.7). Do this multiple times m, and you get z_i’s

b) Ask the LLM what’s the y of (x,z_i) for each z_i and predict y^\hat{y}y^​﻿ as the majority. Temperature 0.

Example: cryobiology in spanish: (Z = translate to different language, write in layman’s words, write as if it was a 5th grade exam, etc)

Why would this work?

→ if Z is an adjustment set ,which means the conditional indp holds, then you are predicting an intervention of x into y and we can prove/we know that it’s more robust to shifts.

→ Q: ICL covariat shift is not clear to me but it should help too. z_i’s for both few shot examples and x.

→ We can also prove that you don’t need too many samples of z_i.
